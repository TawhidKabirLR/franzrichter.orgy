🔒
Zero data collection. Zero exceptions.
Every PDF you open is processed entirely inside your browser. No file contents, no metadata, no usage signals, and no personal information of any kind ever leave your device or touch our servers — because we have no servers receiving your data.
📄 Your Files
PDF files you upload are loaded directly into your browser's memory using the Web File API. They are never uploaded to any server, never written to disk by us, and never transmitted over any network connection. The file exists only in your browser tab's memory for the duration of your session.
📍 Location Data
We never request, read, or store your location. This tool does not use the Geolocation API, does not log your IP address, and does not infer your location from any signal. Where you are in the world is entirely unknown to us and always will be.
🔄 Session & Refresh
When you refresh, close your tab, or navigate away, everything is permanently gone. Your file, all redactions, all annotations, and all session state are destroyed instantly. This is not a warning — it is a by-design privacy guarantee. We cannot recover it because we never held it.
📊 Analytics & Tracking
There are no analytics scripts, no tracking pixels, no third-party SDKs, and no telemetry of any kind embedded in this tool. We do not know how many people use it, what files they process, what tools they use, or how long their sessions last. We deliberately chose not to know.
🍪 Cookies & Storage
This tool sets no cookies and writes nothing to localStorage or sessionStorage. Your browser's storage is untouched before, during, and after use. There is nothing to clear because nothing was ever written.
⬇️ Exported Files
When you export a redacted PDF, the file is generated entirely in your browser using pdf-lib and downloaded directly to your device via a temporary object URL that is immediately revoked. We never see the output. The download goes from your browser to your disk — we are not in that path.
● Our Commitment
"We built this tool with a single principle: the contents of your documents are yours alone. Privacy is not a feature we added — it is the architecture we chose from the start. The safest data is data that was never collected."
